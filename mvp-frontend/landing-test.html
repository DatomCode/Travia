Platform Features

Built for the
urgency of fresh.

Signature Feature
🤝
Farm Clusters
The most innovative feature on Travia. Nearby fish and snail farmers are automatically grouped into clusters. When multiple farmers have orders going the same direction, one shared truck handles all deliveries — and the cost is split proportionally.
Reduces logistics cost by up to 70% for smallholder farmers who can't afford solo delivery.
⏱️
Freshness Timer
Every listing displays a real-time countdown from the moment of harvest. Buyers know exactly how fresh the product is. Farmers with fresher stock get priority placement.
📊
Price Intelligence
Real-time and historical market prices for catfish, tilapia, snails and more. No more guessing. Farmers negotiate from knowledge.
🗓️
Pre-Order System
Buyers book upcoming harvests in advance. Farmers know exactly who is buying before they even harvest. Guaranteed income, zero uncertainty.
