Signature Feature

Farm Clusters

The most innovative feature on Travia. Nearby fish and snail farmers are automatically grouped into clusters. When multiple farmers have orders going the same direction, one shared truck handles all deliveries.

Reduces logistics cost by up to 70% for smallholder farmers.

Freshness Timer

Every listing displays a real-time countdown from the moment of harvest. Buyers know exactly how fresh the product is. Farmers with fresher stock get priority placement.

Price Intelligence

Real-time and historical market prices for catfish, tilapia, snails and more. No more guessing. Farmers negotiate from a position of data-backed knowledge.

Pre-Order System

Buyers book upcoming harvests in advance. Farmers know exactly who is buying before they even harvest. Guaranteed income, zero uncertainty.
